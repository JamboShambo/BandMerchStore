Firenze Rocks Festival 2020

Buy Now

The first edition of Firenze Rocks took place in 2017, when Placebo, Aerosmith and System of a Down attended the festival. In 2018, in front of over 210,000 spectators, The Kills, Foo Fighters, Guns N’ Roses, Helloween, Iron Maiden, Judas Priest and Ozzy Osbourne appeared on stage. Pinkpop Festival 2020

Buy Now

At the festivalsite of Pinkpop, you will enjoy over 50 top acts on 5 stages (open air and indoors) in three days. Next to the main site, you will find smaller areas such as the Kalm Aan Laan (Take It Easy Lane) or Garden of Love with more diversity in food and drinks and a place to relax. At the festival market, you will find a unique offer of bohemian, vintage, boots, bags, shirts (kids as well), sunglasses, hats, jewelry and even more! It’s mostly a place of designs by creative exhibitors. We will see you in the Netherlands!

Ejekt Festival 2020

Buy Now

Ejekt Festival is a music festival that takes place annually at Plateia Nerou in Athens, Greece. The festival first took place in 2005 and since then has become one of the leading music festivals of Greece. In the past years, the festival has hosted the biggest names in rock such as Nick Cave and The Bad Seeds, Muse, The Killers, Kasabian, Editors and many more. Add us to the cast cos here we come!
